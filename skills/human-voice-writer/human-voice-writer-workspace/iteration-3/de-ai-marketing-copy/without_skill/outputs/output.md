The way teams work together is changing fast, and the tools you pick matter — but they're only part of the picture. What really counts is building a setup where people can actually do their best work without fighting against clunky systems or disconnected apps.

This applies whether you're running a five-person startup or managing hundreds of people across departments. AI-powered workflows can take a lot of the repetitive, time-consuming stuff off your plate — things like routing approvals, organizing information, or keeping projects on track — so your team spends more time on work that actually moves the needle.

The result? People get more done, and they have room to think creatively instead of just keeping up.
